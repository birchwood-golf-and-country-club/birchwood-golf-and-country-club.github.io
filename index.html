Clustered around picturesque Buck Lake Creek just west of Lindale, the Birchwood Golf & Country Club is a nine hole golf course with a Par 35.

The golf course's nine holes cross the creek on no less than four fairways and the creek is in play on three others. When water isn't a concern players have to contend with strategic tree placements and the course's unforgiving rough on what is one of Alberta's more challenging nine hole layouts. After your game, a relaxing stop at the licensed restaurant will be what you need before you settle down in the Birchwood Campground.
